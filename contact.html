Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A brand identity takes 3–4 weeks, a website 6–10 weeks, and full campaigns run on monthly retainers. We'll give you a precise timeline in our proposal.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We have clients in UAE, Singapore, UK, and the US. All our processes are remote-friendly with async collaboration tools and scheduled video check-ins.

What's your minimum engagement budget?

Our starter packages begin at ₹25,000/month for retainers. For one-time projects, minimum project budgets start at ₹40,000 depending on scope.

Do you offer equity or deferred-payment deals?

For select early-stage startups, we offer hybrid models. This is reviewed case-by-case. Reach out and let's explore what works for both sides.

Will I have a dedicated point of contact?

Yes — every client gets a dedicated Account Manager who acts as your single point of contact and is reachable via email, WhatsApp, and Slack.

What industries do you specialize in?

We've worked across fintech, e-commerce, retail, real estate, SaaS, food & beverage, education, and healthcare. We adapt our strategies to each industry's unique dynamics.
