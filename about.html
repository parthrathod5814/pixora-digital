Our Story

We Are Pixora — Born Digital, Built Different.

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Morbi, Pixora Digital started as a two-person idea fueled by coffee, code, and a relentless belief that great brands deserve great digital experiences.

Today we're a full-service digital agency working with startups, scale-ups, and established enterprises across India and globally.

To empower businesses of every size with world-class digital strategies, creative execution, and measurable results that fuel sustainable growth in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted creative technology partner in South Asia — known for our integrity, innovation, and the extraordinary outcomes we deliver for clients across industries.

Our Journey

Building Something Lasting

2024

The Beginning

Two co-founders with a shared dream and a rented co-working desk in Morbi. Pixora Digital is officially incorporated with 2 founding clients.

2025

First Big Break

Landed our first enterprise client — a 50-store retail chain — and grew to a team of 8. Delivered a full brand overhaul and e-commerce platform in 90 days.

2025

Scaling Fast

Crossed ₹1 Cr revenue, expanded to 20 team members, and launched our proprietary marketing analytics dashboard. Named "Top 10 Agencies to Watch" by Digital Media India.

2026

Global Reach

Expanded services to UAE and Southeast Asia. Won the Foxglove Award for Best Digital Campaign. Team grew to 35+ and we moved into our own studio space.

2026-Future

The Present & Beyond

50+ team members, 150+ projects delivered, new AI-integrated service offerings, and a commitment to helping 500 brands grow digitally by 2027.
